On Animation Studios

ON est le studio d’animation indépendant du groupe ON Kids & Family, un studio d'animation européen d'envergure, producteur de séries télévisées et de longs métrages à portée internationale. Le studio rassemble aujourd'hui des talents techniques et artistiques exceptionnels au sein de ses sites de Paris, Montréal et Los Angeles.

Installé depuis 2015 à Montréal, ON a produit et créé le pipeline des films Mune: Gardien de la lune et Le Petit Prince, le plus grand succès mondial pour un film d’animation français.

ON travaille aujourd’hui sur Playmobil le film, ainsi que sur 2 autres superproductions d’animation : Badalisc et Slumberland.

Lend me your comb : faster grooming with an artist adaptive software

ON Animation Studios Technical Paper

Jérémy Ringard - 07-2016

In this paper, we present the solution we developed at ON Animation Studios to assist the Character FX department on the production of Mune and The Little Prince. Our goal was to design a tool that adapts to the artists rather than a solution that requires learning and practice. To do this, we provide the grooming artists the freedom to choose the interaction mode they feel the most comfortable with. Therefore, the grooming system we wrote provides three different approaches to create hair: curve sculpting, volume modeling, and sketch-based. While being different, these approaches remains compatible and can be mixed together depending of the artists’ needs.

Isolate any object with a perfect antialiasing

OpenEXR/Id

R.Chikh,C. Corvazier, B. Legros - 06-2016

We present a new storage scheme for computer graphic images based on OpenEXR 2.Using such EXR/Id files, the compositing artist can isolate an object selection (by picking them or using a regular expression to match their names) and color corrects them with no edge artefact, which was not possible to achieve without rendering the object selection on its own layer. Using this file format avoids going back and forth between the rendering and the compositing departments because no mask image or layering are needed anymore.

The technique is demonstrated in an open source software suite, including a library to read and write the EXR/Id files and an OpenFX plug-in which generates the images in any compositing software.
